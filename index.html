The LMU Computer Science Senior Projects

LMU was one of the first universities to require both individual and group-based semester-long projects for the B.S. degree in Computer Science. Here's a list of these projects.

Help us complete our history ! If you see your name in this list and we have your project details wrong, please send a pull request. Or send us a pull request with missing or other incorrect details. And do tell your friends who have lost touch to check out the list, too.
